In a very productive, desperate, and fun attempt to cope with my existential and adulthood crisis, I decided to strategically invest my time in creating a state-of-the-art website that would revolutionize how high-school students can make well rounded and data driven decisions for their university choices.

{/* truncate */}
Here's collegesaurus, the proper and simplified guide for college applications in Lebanon.

It would be cool to gather all the Lebanese college application information in one place, but it would be much cooler to create a network of university alumni guiding and helping high schoolers in their career journey.
