Growing up in Akkar, I never had a clear roadmap for what comes after high school. No one in my circle had navigated international scholarships or studied abroad, and the information that did exist was scattered, outdated, or locked behind people who happened to know people. I figured things out the hard way — and I got lucky.

{/* truncate */}

I studied in Budapest on a scholarship, graduated, and now I'm working here — thousands of kilometers from home. I carry that same feeling Hashem described: the urge to give back. I know firsthand how a single piece of the right information at the right time can change someone's entire trajectory. That's what Collegesaurus is about for me — making sure the next student from Akkar or from anywhere in Lebanon, doesn't have to rely on luck alone.

If I can help even one person avoid the confusion I went through, or discover an opportunity they didn't know existed, then this is worth every minute. Reach out anytime — I'm here to help.
