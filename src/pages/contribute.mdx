# Contribute

Collegesaurus is run by volunteers, and anyone can help keep it
accurate. You don't need to know how to code, and most of the
options below take less than five minutes.

## Read the source

Every page on this site is generated from a folder on Google Drive,
and we rebuild the site from it every night around 7 AM Beirut time.
You can browse that folder yourself — every university and
scholarship lives there as a plain `.docx` or `.xlsx` file.

[**Open the Drive folder →**](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1ZMZAqv4R2OzMk2MaxuB7cMjejBzwcla4)

It's open for reading without signing in. To make changes, see
*Become a regular contributor* below.

## Spotted something wrong?

If a tuition figure looks off, a deadline has shifted, a contact has
moved, or anything else doesn't add up — tell us. You don't have to
fix it yourself; just point at what's wrong and where you saw better
information.

[**Flag a correction →**](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScUnf_qsTZXRX5CKP1KkK_Yy5VuhkUBjo988FNbqSzzYz301w/viewform?usp=dialog)

## Missing a scholarship or university?

If you know of a scholarship Lebanese students can apply to, or a
school that should be on the site, you can add it directly. You'll
download a small template, fill it in, and upload it through the
form.

- A new **scholarship** needs two files — `info.docx` (English) and
  `info.ar.docx` (Arabic).
  Templates: [scholarship templates folder](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1hGsGdrWj-LZht2INGb-77JBWE3bHap1b).
- A new **university** needs four files — `info.docx`,
  `info.ar.docx`, `majors.xlsx`, and `majors.ar.xlsx`.
  Templates: [university templates folder](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/16z_utAOa9MFFfdaeX_eaivw8Ga6OryK0).

> Uploading files requires you to sign in with Google.

[**Submit a new scholarship or university →**](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScUnf_qsTZXRX5CKP1KkK_Yy5VuhkUBjo988FNbqSzzYz301w/viewform?usp=dialog)

## Become a regular contributor

If you'd like ongoing edit access — for example, you're an
admissions officer or a volunteer who wants to keep a specific page
current — email
**[hashemkhoder1@gmail.com](mailto:hashemkhoder1@gmail.com?subject=Collegesaurus%20contribution)**
with subject `Collegesaurus contribution`. Tell us:

- Who you are and what organisation (if any) you represent.
- Which page or folder you'd like to edit.
- A line on why.

We'll add you as an Editor on the right Drive folder, and your edits
will flow into the live site at the next nightly resync.

## What happens next

- The maintainer reviews submissions every few days.
- Verifiable updates go live within an hour or two of approval.
- You're credited the way you picked in the form — by name,
  anonymously, or not at all.

Thank you. Every fix, however small, helps a student make a
better-informed decision.
